About Me

Hello! My name is Dan and I am a front-end web developer with a new found passion for coding. As a former strength and conditioning coach with a Master's in Sport Science, my desire to code began a few months prior to the 2020 Covid-19 pandemic. With declining interest and enthusiasm of coaching athletes, I made the decision to pursue web development and make learning to code my priority. The prospect of pursuing a career that allowed me to work in an always rapidly developing field excited me. The opportunity to develop streamlined software while always pushing forward with advancements in technology by learning and obtaining new knowledge presented challenges that I find enjoyable and never monotonous! While the pandemic of 2020 is unfortunate, it provided me an invaluable opportunity to pursue web development with no looking back since!
